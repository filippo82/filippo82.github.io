About me

Summary

I have 10 years of experience in computational and theoretical Geophysics. I am passionate about data science, education, and teaching. Currently, I am a Solutions Engineer at Squirro and, previously, I was a researcher in the Exploration and Environmental Geophysics group at ETH Zürich. I co-supervised PhD students and MSc students, and taught a BSc class on geophysical methods. In my spare time, I run, bike, cook, build a Raspberry Pi cluster, and update my personal website.

You can find out more about me on my curriculum vitae.
